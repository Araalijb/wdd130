Purpose

The website's primary purpose will be to present my skills, achievements, and past work to potential employers or clients. It will serve as a professional online portfolio that highlights my expertise and capabilities in the field.

Audience

The intended audience for this website includes potential employers, clients, or collaborators in the professional context. It's tailored for individuals or organizations looking to assess my qualifications and experience in areas such as writing, design, and content creation. This website will also offer a contact section for business inquiries, making it easy for prospective employers or clients to get in touch.
